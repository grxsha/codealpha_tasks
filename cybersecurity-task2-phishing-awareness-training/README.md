# Phishing Awareness Training

## Objective

This project aims to educate users about phishing attacks, social engineering techniques, and safe online practices. It explains how to recognize phishing emails, fake websites, and common attacker tactics while providing practical tips to stay secure.

---

## Topics Covered

- What is Phishing?
- Types of Phishing Attacks
- How to Recognize Phishing Emails
- Identifying Fake Websites
- Social Engineering Techniques
- Best Practices for Staying Safe
- Real-World Examples
- Interactive Quiz

---

## Learning Outcomes

After completing this training, users will be able to:

- Identify phishing attempts.
- Recognize suspicious emails and websites.
- Understand social engineering attacks.
- Protect personal and financial information.
- Apply cybersecurity best practices.
